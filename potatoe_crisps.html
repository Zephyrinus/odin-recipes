Potatoe crisps


Ingredients

  • 1 and a half potatoes per person
  • half a stick of butter
  • italian seasoning to taste
  • onion slices
  • garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes into thin, see through slices.
  2. Chop onion.
  3. Chop 3~ cloves of garlic.
  4. Melt a quarter stick of butter into pan.
  5. Set stove to medium and cook potatoes until browning. Add butter and stir as needed.
  6. Add onion slices and garlic pieces.
  7. Enjoy.
